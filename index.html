    My Hobby Explained

    Reading and Writing has been my hobby just recently. I always enjoy doing it in my leisure time, plus, the pandemic, has given me enough time to practice it even more. First, I didn't really like reading becuase I thought it had to many hard words, don't even think about writing because I couldn't write sentences that could make sense. Then, I started reading the Geronimo Stiltion series which was really amusing to read and all the new vocabulary didn't seem hard. So, the more I read, the more strong my vocabulary became and I began writing the words in my vocabulary notebook. After that, I knew the most common words so I went back to the books I thought were hard and began reading them. They were way better than I thought they would be! As I read, the more words apperared on my notebook. Now, I have over 60 words and I revise them almost every single day! Moreover, writing became one of my hobbies after I started reading books. I just used to stroll around the house and think of a new sentence with perfect vocabulary tha would go with it. I practice my writng skills everyday and it just seems that I get better and better, as I continue doing it.
